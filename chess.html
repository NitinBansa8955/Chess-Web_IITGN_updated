Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers!

The IITGN Chess Club is a student-led organization dedicated to promoting the game of chess among the IITGN community. We organize various events, tournaments, and activities related to chess.

The club is open to all members of the IITGN community, including students, faculty, and staff who have an interest in playing chess or learning more about the game.

No, players of all skill levels are welcome to join the club. Whether you're a beginner looking to learn the basics or an experienced player seeking challenging opponents, there's a place for you in our club.

The club organizes a variety of events, including friendly matches, blitz tournaments, simultaneous exhibitions, chess workshops, and more. We aim to provide opportunities for players to improve their skills and enjoy the game in a fun and supportive environment.

Information about upcoming events and tournaments, including registration details, will be posted on our website and announced through club communication channels. Simply follow the instructions provided to sign up and participate.

No, membership in the IITGN Chess Club is free for all members of the IITGN community. We believe in making chess accessible to everyone and do not charge any membership fees.
